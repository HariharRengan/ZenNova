What We Do

ZenNova is a smart wellness app designed to enhance sleep, nutrition, and mental well-being. It tracks sleep patterns, creates personalized schedules, and offers guided meditations for relaxation. Using your fridge inventory, it generates tailored diet and exercise plans. With an AI companion always available for support, ZenNova helps you stay balanced and healthy.
